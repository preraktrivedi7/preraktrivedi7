## 💡 What I care about

<details>
<summary>Click to expand</summary>

<br/>

The hard part of AI product work is not just calling a model. It is making the system useful, reliable, measurable, and safe enough for real workflows. That means defining what success looks like before launch, measuring failures rather than demos, deciding what should and should not be automated, designing fallback and escalation paths, managing cost and latency, and turning field signal into product improvements.

</details>
